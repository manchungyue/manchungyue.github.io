About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University. I previously worked as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Data and Systems Engineering at The University of Hong Kong and as a Research Associate in the group of Wolfram Wiesemann at Imperial College London. I received my Ph.D. in Systems Engineering and Engineering Management under the supervision of Anthony Man-Cho So, and my B.Sc. in Mathematics, both from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

My research focuses on continuous optimization and its interplay with machine learning, operations research, data science, and signal processing.
