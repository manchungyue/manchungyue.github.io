About Me

I'm currently a postdoc at Imperial College London, working with Wolfram Wiesemann. I received my B.Sc. in Mathematics in 2012 and Ph.D. in Systems Engineering and Engineering Management under the supervision of Anthony Man-Cho So in 2017, both from The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

Research Interests

Optimization
  • Nonlinear Optimization, Second-Order Methods, Error Bound Theory, Robust Optimization
Signal Processing
  • Sparse/Low-Rank Recovery, Group Synchronization Problem, Beamforming in Wireless Communication Network
